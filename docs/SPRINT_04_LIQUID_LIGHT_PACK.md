# Sprint 04 — Liquid Light Pack

## Purpose

Sprint 04 expands InfinityLens369 beyond high-intensity trip modes by adding a softer visual family. The Liquid Light Pack is meant for long listening sessions, ambient tracks, meditation-like music, livestream backgrounds, projection walls, and people who want beauty without constant hyperspeed.

## Design principles

- Soft motion before intensity.
- Smooth audio response before raw beat punch.
- Glow and refraction before hard glitch.
- Beautiful presets first; standalone shader modes after the best presets are proven.
- Keep Safe Mode and Audio Engine v2 behavior intact.

## Preset family

### Aurora Veil

Base route: Cosmic Drift

Suggested settings:

- palette: Aurora Phi
- audio reactive: true
- pressure: 0.18–0.26
- audio drive: 0.10–0.18
- bass impact: low-medium
- mids motion: medium
- high sparkle: medium-soft
- beat punch: low
- response: low/smooth
- glow: medium-high

Feel: slow curtains of neon aurora light drifting over a cosmic field.

### Liquid Glass

Base route: Acid Melt

Suggested settings:

- palette: Abyss Cyan or Violet Gold Duality
- audio reactive: true
- pressure: 0.16–0.24
- audio drive: 0.10–0.18
- bass impact: low
- mids motion: medium
- high sparkle: low-medium
- beat punch: very low
- response: very smooth
- glow: medium

Feel: refractive glass, soft bending, underwater lens flow.
